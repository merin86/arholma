Hostels

hostels to choose from during your stay

Arholma Nord

Arholma Nord is a hostel on the north side of the island with several different houses with rooms. Shower and toilet are adjacent to the rooms and shared with other guests. They have a restaurant and offer several different activities, such as zipline, kayaking and "Batteri Arholma", an old Swedish defense facility that has now become a museum.

Book room

Bull August

Bull August is a hostel in the middle of the island. They offer breakfast in the mornings as an option. You cook your own food in the communal kitchen and also share the shower and WC with other visitors. Bull August rents out bicycles to visitors and has a shared sauna that you can book.

Book room

Österhamns Pensionat

Österhamns pensionat is a hostel in the middle of the island on the eastern side. They have a cafe where they serve hot and cold dishes, as well as full rights for serving alcohol. Breakfast buffet is included in the room price. Österhamns pensionat offers various weekend packages with activities such as Yoga, dancing and Christmas table.
